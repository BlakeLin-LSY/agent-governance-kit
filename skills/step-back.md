# Skill: step-back

Stop adding; check whether you are still aimed at the goal.

## When to use
When a task has grown heavy, when ceremony is outweighing output, or before a
pivot. The counter-habit to building: pruning.

## How it works
1. **Restate the actual goal** in one line.
2. **List what currently exists** against that goal.
3. **Mark each item** keep / cut / defer.
4. **Issue a pivot directive** — the smallest next step that serves the goal.

## Output
A short directive: what to stop doing, what to keep, and the single next action.

## Principle
"I built guardrails and a habit of pruning them" beats "I built a lot."
